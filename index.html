About Me

Hi, I’m Aidan Dougan — a senior at SCAD studying Interactive Design and Game Development, with a passion for game systems, mechanics design, and creative problem solving.

I’m especially interested in how gameplay systems can shape emotion, narrative, and player experience. Whether it's building procedural environments, designing core mechanics, or developing intuitive player interactions, I’m drawn to the intersection of logic and creativity.

Right now, I’m focused on growing as a systems designer by exploring new tools, iterating on new ideas, and learning from both indie experiments and AAA principles. I love diving deep into how things work — and how they could work better.
